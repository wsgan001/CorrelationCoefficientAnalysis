French Presidential Elections Underway

Over in France the voters are casting their ballots in a presidential election.
On the ballot is incumbent Nicolas Sarkozy and nine other candidates.
The first-round of voting will decide which TWO candidates will run for the presidential seat.
Opinion polls suggest that Sarkozy and the Socialist party challenger, Francois Hollande, will make it to the second round.
The next round of voting is scheduled for May 6th.
Key election issues are the economy and jobs as France has an unemployment rate of 10-percent.
The presidential term in France is for five years.
