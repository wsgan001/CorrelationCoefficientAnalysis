Brazil not yet chosen IMF candidate to back-Mantega

Brazil has not yet decidedwhich candidate to support for the leadership of the International Monetary Fund, Finance Minister Guido Mantega said on Wednesday.

Mexico's central bank chief, Agustin Carstens, arrived in Brazil hoping to win crucial backing in Latin America for his bid to the lead the IMF.

French Finance Minister Christine Lagarde, who is also in the running, met with Brazilian officials earlier this week.
