IMF: MERKEL, LAGARDE OK BUTACCEPTS FUTURE NON EU HEAD

(AGI)Singapore - French Finance Minister, Christine Lagarde is a qualified candidate as the next International Monetary Fund chief but also non Ue candidates should have a chance in the future, said German Chancellor Angela Merkel at a forum in Singapore. "If I look at the personality of Christine Lagarde, as a finance minister, she enjoys an excellent reputation worldwide," Merkel said, "and is the ideal embodiment of economic and political experience." "I hope emerging countries as well as other countries take an objective and unbiased look at her although they too should obviously have a chance," the German Chancellor said. . .
