Brazil's Mantega Says Emerging Markets Don't Have IMF Candidate

Read more: http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/g/a/2011/05/29/bloomberg1376-LM0TBU6S972A01-4QPEJ8JB0MF98KEBD0JI27Q2P7.DTL#ixzz1OJaWI5o5


May 30 (Bloomberg) -- Emerging markets don't have a preferred candidate to head the International Monetary Fund, Brazilian Finance Minister Guido Mantega said.

Mantega, speaking to reporters in Brasilia alongside French Finance Minister Christine Lagarde, said Brazil would listen to the proposals of every candidate before deciding who to support.

Lagarde, who announced her candidacy May 25, has won endorsements from across Europe and also plans to travel to China to broaden support for her bid among developing countries.
