LAGARDE FLYS TO BRASIL TO WIN SUPPORT FOR HER IMF BID

Brazilia - Christine Lagarde arrived today in Brazil, her first stop on her tour across the most influential emerging countries to secure their support in her race for the IMF top job. France's Finance Minister will travel to India and China and then to Russia and Saudi Arabia. Her only official opponent now is Mexico's candidate Augustin Carstens, governor of Mexico's Central Bank.
