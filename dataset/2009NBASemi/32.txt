Lakers Beat Nuggets to Take 2-1 Lead in NBA Conference Finals

May 24 (Bloomberg) -- Kobe Bryant scored 41 points, including eight in the final 68 seconds, to lead the Los Angeles Lakers to a 103-97 win over the Denver Nuggets, who snapped a 16-game winning streak at home.

The win gave the Lakers a 2-1 advantage in the National Basketball Association Western Conference finals.

Bryant hit a 3-pointer from 24 feet out at 1:08 to give the Lakers a 96-95 lead and added five free throws to clinch the victory. Pau Gasol added 20 points and 11 rebounds.
